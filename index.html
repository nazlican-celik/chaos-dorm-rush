Dream Items (Daydream Only)

  • 💎 Dream Crystal: +40 Pts
  • ✨ Fantasy Star: +50 Pts

  • ⏰ Reality Check: +10 Fcs, Exit early

Hazards & Obstacles

  • 🪫 Low Battery: -15 Focus
  • 📱 Social Trap: -20 Focus, Reset Combo
  • 📅 Deadline Monster: -25 Focus
  • ☁️ Overthinking: -10 Focus (Slide!)
  • 😴 Sleep Demon: Slows speed briefly
  • 👻 Project Ghost: -15 Fcs, Reset Combo

Controls: Space / ▲ Jump  |  ▼ Slide  |  P Pause  |  R Restart  |  M Mute Music

Controls Cheat Sheet

  • Space or ▲ Jump (avoid low obstacles)
  • ▼ Slide (avoid clouds/trap apps, or fall fast)
  • M Toggle Music On/Off
  • P Pause / Resume  |  R Restart

Audio Options

Press M to mute/unmute the chiptune synthesizer at any time. Music plays only after starting.
